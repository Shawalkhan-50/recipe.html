Fried Noodles🍜

👉Ingredients

  • Noodles: 450g fresh or dried noodles (Shanghai, chow mein, or ramen).
  • Sauce: 3 tbsp light soy sauce, 2.5 tbsp dark soy sauce, 2 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp sesame oil, 4 tbsp water/stock.
  • Aromatics: 6 cloves garlic (sliced), 3 green onions.
  • Vegetables/Protein: Bean sprouts, choy sum, or sliced onions.

Instruction🍽

  1. Prepare Noodles: Boil noodles until just cooked, then rinse immediately with cold water to remove starch and prevent sticking.
  2. Make Sauce: Combine soy sauces, sugar, sesame oil, and stock in a bowl.
  3. Stir-fry: Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Sauté aromatics (garlic, onion) for 1 minute.
  4. Combine: Add noodles and vegetables, tossing for 2-3 minutes. Pour in sauce and toss until noodles are evenly coated and charred, about 3-4 minutes.
